02 / Architecture

Found Space

Excavated beneath the museum, following the contours of the MTR Airport Express tunnel — just 1.5m below ground. A cavernous space for monumental installations.

B2 · Underground

The Podium

A cantilevered horizontal mass housing 33 galleries across 17,000m² of exhibition space. Three cinemas, the Mediatheque, and a Learning Hub live within.

G–3F · 65,000 m² total

The Tower

Rising 65 metres, the slender tower completes the inverted-T silhouette. Research, dining, and the M+ Lounge on Level 11 with harbour views.

4–20F · 20 storeys

Engineering

Five mega-trusses by Arup span above the railway tunnels, allowing the museum to float without loading the infrastructure below.

Herzog & de Meuron + TFP Farrells + Arup
